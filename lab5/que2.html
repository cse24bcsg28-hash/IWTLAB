Welcome to Online Exam System

Online examination systems represent a significant modernization in educational assessment, replacing traditional paper-based methods with secure, web-based, or application-based platforms. These systems offer benefits such as immediate results, reduced logistical costs, and enhanced accessibility for remote candidates.

Key functionalities include AI-powered proctoring to monitor for suspicious behavior via webcams and browser lockdown features to maintain integrity. Furthermore, they allow for flexible scheduling and automated grading of multiple-choice questions, thereby reducing the administrative burden on instructors.
